About Farlands SMP

Farlands SMP is a community-driven Minecraft survival server focused on exploration, building, and adventure. Our goal is to create a fun, fair, and challenging world for players to explore beyond the ordinary.

Journey into the unknown and discover the legendary Far Lands, where new biomes, dungeons, and mysteries await. Build your empire, trade with friends, and survive in a fully player-driven world.

Our server offers a mix of classic SMP gameplay and unique custom features that enhance the survival experience without giving unfair advantages. Whether you're a builder, explorer, or warrior, there's something here for everyone.

Server Features

Survival Gameplay

Experience vanilla survival with a few quality-of-life enhancements for a balanced experience.

Player Economy

Trade, sell, and buy in a dynamic economy powered by players themselves.

Custom Mobs

Face unique enemies and rare creatures that make exploration more challenging and rewarding.

Lifesteal or Heart Steaking

You can steal another hearts and can lose too your hearts #play safe.

Events & Challenges

Participate in weekly events, competitions, and seasonal challenges for rewards.

Community-Focused

Join a friendly, active community where collaboration and creativity are encouraged.
