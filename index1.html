About Our SMP

Farlands SMP is a community-driven Minecraft survival server where players explore untouched terrain, build powerful empires, survive harsh biomes, and discover many secrets as they journey toward the legendary Far Lands. No pay-to-win. No unfair advantages. Just pure survival.
