Server Rules

These rules keep Farlands SMP safe, fair, and enjoyable for everyone.

General Rules

  • Be respectful toward all players.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or toxicity.
  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • No spamming or chat flooding.
  • No impersonating staff or players.

Survival World Rules

  • Do not destroy public areas or community builds.
  • Do not trap portals, bases, or players.
  • Do not excessively lag the server with farms.
  • Claim your land to protect your builds.
  • Use farms responsibly & avoid chunk-loading exploits.

PvP & Combat Rules

  • No hacking, modded clients, or unfair advantages.
  • No combat logging during PvP fights.
  • No teleport trapping or home trapping.
  • Keep PvP fair — no spawn killing.

Allowed Mods

  • Optifine / Sodium / Performance mods.
  • Minimaps (NO player/entity radar).
  • Shaders.
  • Resource packs.

Banned Mods

  • KillAura, AutoClickers, Aimbot.
  • X-Ray texture packs or mods.
  • Fly hacks, velocity cheats, Anti-KB.
  • Duplication glitches or exploits.

Punishment System

  • Warning → First minor offense.
  • Kick → Continued disruption.
  • Temporary Ban → Moderate rule-breaking.
  • Permanent Ban → Severe or repeated offenses.

Staff Guidelines

  • Staff must remain unbiased and fair.
  • No abusing permissions.
  • Always provide proof when issuing bans.
  • Treat all players equally.
